Privacy Policy


As a visitor to the eosc.prominence.cloud website
Individual people are not tracked. As a visitor to this website:
  • No personal information is collected,
  • No information such as cookies is stored in the browser,
  • No information is shared with, sent to or sold to third-parties,
  • No information is shared with advertising companies.

As a user of the Prominence service
An EGI Check-in account is required to access Prominence. Identification and behvourial data collected when accessing Prominence consists of:
  • The pseudononymous EGI User ID,
  • IP address,
  • Timestamp.
No personal information such as name or email address is collected.
